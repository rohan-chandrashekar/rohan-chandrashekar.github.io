Hi, I'm Rohan.

I recently graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from PES University and completed a summer internship at Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE).

During my internship, I immersed myself in various advanced topics, including AI for cybersecurity, and BurpSuite for Web LLM attacks. I developed a use case for an AI-powered chatbot to summarize technical cybersecurity documents and integrated the NIST framework to strengthen AI security measures.

Currently, I am working full-time with HPE’s SQuest framework, which is built on Red Hat’s Ansible Automation Platform, offering a comprehensive Everything-as-a-Service (EaaS) experience. My focus areas are AI for Cybersecurity and Automation.

In parallel, my research interests span Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, NLP, and Cybersecurity.

For more details on my publications and projects, you can view my portfolio and resume.
